About Devour

I started to write my cookbook, Devour, about 3 years ago. I've only created and documented roughly 20 recipes. Aside from that, Devour lives in a Google Doc. I chose this specific turkey burger recipe for a few reasons.

  1. It's the best burger without a bun I've ever had.
  2. It's a healthy but delicious meal.
  3. Who doesn't love quacamole?

I've made this burger for people with all different types of eating habits, and have successfully pleased them all. Even a non-guac eater enjoyed it!
