Turkey Burger Recipe

Prep Time 10 minutes
Total Time 20 minutes
Servings 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground turkey
  • 1 red onion, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, diced
  • 1 orange, yellow or red pepper, diced
  • 1 jalapeno, diced
  • 2 Avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 1 cup plain greek yogurt
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 lime
  • 2 tbsp avocado oil
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp garlic salt
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 tbsp Sriracha

Instructions

Guacamole

  1. Dice red onion, tomatoes, pepper, and jalapeno.
  2. In a medium mixing bowl, mash the avocados, leaving some small chunks.
  3. Add the squuzed lime juice, 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp pepper, 1 tsp garlic salt, 2 tsp cumin and 1 tsp paprika. Mix well.
  4. Add 1/2 cup of diced red onions, diced cherry tomatoes, and diced jalapeno. Gently mix in.
  5. Refrigerate for 1 hour, or until the turkey burgers are finished.

Turkey Burger

  1. In a large mixing bowl, add and mix together ground turkey, 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp pepper, 1 tsp garlic salt, 1 tsp paprika, and 1 tsp cayenne pepper.
  2. Add 2 tbsp of avocado oil to a large skillet on medium-high heat.
  3. Place a clean plate or cuttig board next to turkey mixture bowl.
  4. Add 1/2 cup of diced red onions and diced peppers to the turkey mixture.
  5. Using your hands, gentlty mix everything together.
  6. Form 4 patties (about 1 1/2 inches thick), placing them on the clean plate or cutting board.
  7. Add 4 patties to heated skillet and brown each side for 2 minutes.
  8. Add 1/2 cup of chicken broth to skillet, squueze approximatley 1 tbsp of Sriracha on top of burgers, cover, and simmer on medium for 5 minutes.
  9. Remove lid, flip burgers, add remaining 1/2 cup of chicken broth to skillet, squueze approximatley 1 tbsp of Sriracha on top of burgers, cover, and simmer on medium for 5 minutes.
