Ellie's Favorite Song

The Police: Every Breath You Take

I chose this song because my Dad used to sing this to me all the time. Up until about 2 years ago, I had no idea that it wasn't a love song. Sting wrote this song about his obsessive lost lover with Big Brother- surveillance and control in mind. This was also in the 80's....I wonder what he thinks about Cookies. (not the kind you eat) So, just in case you thought it was a love song too, I thought I'd share.
